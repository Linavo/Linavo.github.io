Social Media & Cyber Security Website


As we transition our entire lives into the digital universe, privacy breaches are bound to be in our foreseeable futures. For this project, I had decided to dedicate social media and cyber security as the main focal point. My contributions to the website were primarily UX design, content creator, and website designer. Juggling these three roles allowed me to gain a better insight of how to properly present a pleasing, yet, informative resource for others to view. As a UX designer, I needed to figure out the best layout and most important information that needed to be displayed.

I was researching on this topic, I purposefully provided information first then put the FAQ’s at the end of the navigation bar. When users first come to my website, they will find that it is extremely simple yet effective because it is easy to use. The FAQ’s were placed at the end of the page for readers to first gather information in the previous navigation links.

For each page, I wrote the information as readable as possible so people of most ages may understand this very important topic. Cyber security is such an important topic in this day and age; as our society is growing closer to having everything become digital, privacy breaches are bound to happen. As a website designer, I first wireframed how I wanted the website to look and then took my visualizations to life with Square Space. Square space allowed me to properly take my prototype to life by using their preset features that were attractive to me.

Social media and cyber security is a major topic that many of us internet users need to be informed on. Making my website not only spreads awareness to others, but it can be readable to a wide variety of audiences.
