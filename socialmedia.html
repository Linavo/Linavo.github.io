Social Media & Cyber Security Website


As social media platforms have become one of our main sources of communication, it is important to be informed on how to protect ourselves from others on the internet. For this project, I decided to dedicate it to both social media and cyber security.

My roles to this website includes being the UX designer, content creator, and website designer. To better relay the information to my readers, I needed to figure out the best layout for my website. For an example, in the navigation bar you might have noticed that the link containing how to protect ourselves from attacks is closer to the end. By providing the background information on how privacy breaches can harm you first, viewers are able to better understand how the prevention works.

For each topic, I made it a priority to have people from most ages understand my writing. Cyber security is such an important topic in this day and age; as our society is growing closer to having everything become digital, privacy breaches are bound to happen. As a website designer, I first wireframed how I wanted the website to look. Squarespace has allowed me to take my prototype to life by using their preset features to build the website.

I created this website because I believe cyber security breaching is a major topic that many of us internet users need to be more informed on.
