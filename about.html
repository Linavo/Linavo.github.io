About Me.

Hello! My name is Lina and I enjoy figuring out solutions to daily problems. My interest in front end development began in product design. I originally started my passion in creating digital experiences by creating wireframes, prototypes, and by conducting usability tests.

After successfully creating prototypes for my classes at Arizona State University, I figured why not take my prototypes a step further? After successfully taking a web development course at a local college, I furthered my education by teaching myself Javascript. My main focus these days is to build functional websites that are also mobile friendly!
