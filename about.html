About Me.

Hello! My name is Lina and I enjoy figuring out solutions to daily problems. My interest in front end development began in product design. I originally started creating wireframes, prototypes, and conducted usability tests in pursuit of UX/UI. After successfully creating prototypes for my classes at Arizona State University, I figured why not take my prototypes a step further? I decided to teach myself HTML, CSS, and Javascript. My main focus these days is to build functional websites that are mobile friendly.
