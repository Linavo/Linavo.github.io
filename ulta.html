Ulta's Content Strategy


Every major corporation always has a great content strategist. Many may take on several roles within the company or be specialized in content strategy itself. For my proposal to Ulta Beauty, I broke down the many factors that contributed to the company's marketing plan. The proposal itself provides the interactor profile, which identifies the general demographic of consumers at the company, and content fitness.

One of my most important tasks was to analyze the company's greatest competitors, Sephora and Sally’s Beauty Supply. For this project, I went on each competitor’s website and compared their features, targeted audiences, and content with Ulta’s. After gaining insight of the various similarities and differences they all have, I was able to make the conclusion that Ulta’s greatest strengths relied on maintaining current trends. Ulta has significantly gained more customers in these past few years due to their wide audience of customers.

For my proposal, I demonstrated great knowledge on how to properly analyze a company and their content strategies.
