Dutch Bro's Website Redesign


Have you ever visited a website and wondered, “Why is it so difficult for me to do what I need?”. Some of your favorite brands might have functioning websites, but many times improvement can make your love for them even greater.

As a user experience designer, my job is to not only research ways to create better usability but to implement those ideas as well. There were a few issues with the original Dutch Bro’s website such as images and blank spaces that had taken up too much room on the website. Another issue that was pointed out was the amount of bright colors that was not appealing to mature audiences. I had kept the color themes more muted for better visibility, and removed excess images to allow the website to look more appeased. I rearranged the empty space to allow users to scroll through the website without feeling like it took forever as well.

By simplifying their current website, it gave it a better interaction and user design.
