NFTGo


As cryptocurrency has exploded in the past decade, NFT’s have become the next big thing. They are known as non-fungible tokens; they represent digital artwork that has monetary worth and may even grant you entry to exclusive memberships. Many people aren’t very aware of what an NFT is or what it offers. NFTGo is dedicated to informing new and current readers on the amazing benefits of joining the virtual world.

I created NFTGo in order to inspire others, and to create a website that my created persona would most likely develop. I created a website that not only provides awareness to NFTs, but a website that informs readers on the most popular NFT chains, latest events in the Los Angeles area, and more. Instead of being a website that promotes purely the latest NFT purchases, it is used as an informational platform for others to connect with one another.

By implementing my web design, technical writing, and user experience skills, I’ve been able to successfully build a website specifically targeted for NFT networking. Each page was carefully constructed to have their own topics for viewers to browse. To engage readers, I was able to display my storytelling skills to keep their attention on each page. Most importantly, I used my user experience skills to determine the usability layout and features of my website.
