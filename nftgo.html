NFTGo


As cryptocurrency has exploded in the past decade, NFT’s have become the next big thing. They are known as 'non-fungible tokens' and are represented by digital artwork. Their purpose is to contain monetary worth and may even grant you entry to exclusive memberships. Many people aren’t aware of what an NFT is or what it has to offer. NFTGo is dedicated to informing readers on the amazing benefits of joining the NFT world.

I created NFTGo in order to inform people of the topic and to create a website that my persona would most likely develop. The website informs readers on the most popular NFT chains, the latest events in the Los Angeles area, and more. Instead of promoting the latest NFT purchases, it is used as an informational platform for others to connect with one another.

By implementing my web design, technical writing, and user experience skills, I’ve been able to successfully build a website specifically targeted for NFT networking. Each page was carefully constructed to have their own topics for viewers to browse. To engage readers, I was able to display my storytelling skills to keep their attention on each page. Most importantly, I used my user experience skills to determine the usability layout and features of my website.
